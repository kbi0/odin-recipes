Dessert

Description

Overkill dessert. Starts with a layer of crushed biscuits with melted butter. Followed by a second layer which is made of cream cheese, melted white chocolate, vanilla and double cream. Finally mixed berries are added on top.

Ingredients

  • biscuits 200g
  • butter 300g
  • cream cheese 300g
  • double cream 200ml
  • white chocolate 150g
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
